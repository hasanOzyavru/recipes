Ingredients:
 - 1 pack of pasta
 - 1 litre water
 - 100 gr butter
 - 1/2 teaspoon salt
 - 1 jar of pesto sauce
 

Directions:
 1. Put the water in to a pot. Heat it up until boiling
 
 2. Put pasta into boiling water. Add salt.

 3. Let pasta becomes soft but not keep it long inside boiling water. 

 4. Empty the water and add butter in to hot pasta.

 5. Serve in plate with pesto sauce.
